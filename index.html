Accounting is the language of business. — Warren Buffett

Souvik Kumar Das joined HOPEDWELL Energy Solutions Pvt Ltd. in 2018 as Head of Accounting & Finance. Prior to joining HOPEDWELL, he led corporate branding, training and marketing initiatives in Maritime Training Sector. Mr. Das holds a Bachelor in Commerce from the University of Calcutta.

In his own words “We want to see more renewable energy in our community and we want to see it sooner rather than later. We believe that by driving the allocation of capital into a community-owned solar power project such as this one, we can make a real change.

HOPEDWELL Energy Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a developer of Renewable Energy power-stations and supplier of solar equipments. We bring the best value to our customers by offering the latest proven advances in Renewable Energy. Renewable Energy can reach to unreachable and grow our India to the fullest”

Being an ECE student, he has been a part of various solar energy projects during his student life and realized that solar energy is a major renewable energy source with the potential to meet many of the challenges facing today’s world.

In his words, “Solar energy is nothing but the radiant energy emitted by Sun.On the average sunny day the sun shines approximately 1,000 watts of energy per square meter, enough energy to power all of our electricity needs and excess. We are devoted about contributing to a world, which does not depend on power from fossil fuels.
